Patrick Bateman's Morning Ritual: The Checklist

"You can always be thinner, look better."

This comprehensive routine is designed for optimal efficiency and a Bateman-esque pursuit of aesthetic perfection, integrating a challenging morning workout and meticulous grooming. Each step is deliberate, maximizing product efficacy and personal presentation.

Goal Wake-Up Time: 4:00 AM (to be at work by 7:30-7:50 AM)

Phase 1: Pre-Shower Preparation & Pre-Workout Boost (4:00 AM - 4:15 AM)
  • 1. Hydration & Energy
    Product(s) Used: Water (Optional: Pre-workout supplement / Small Carb Source)
    Detailed Action: Immediately upon waking, drink a large glass of water. Optionally, consume a pre-workout supplement or a small, easily digestible carb source (e.g., half a banana) for gym fuel.
    Frequency: Daily
  • 2. Cryotherapy De-Puffing
    Product(s) Used: Skin Gym - Stainless Steel Ice Roller
    Detailed Action: Retrieve from freezer. Roll *meticulously* over the entire face, jawline, and under-eye area. Focus on lymphatic drainage, moving outwards and downwards.
    Frequency: Daily
  • 3. Optional Pre-Workout Skin Prep
    Product(s) Used: Paula's Choice - SKIN BALANCING Oil-Reducing Cleanser (or Athea Oil Cleanser)
    Detailed Action: If skin feels particularly greasy after sleep and before sweating at the gym, perform a quick cleanse. Rinse thoroughly.
    Frequency: As needed (e.g., if skin feels overly oily)
Phase 2: Gym Session & Quick Transition (4:15 AM - 5:35 AM)
  • 4. Gym Workout (Full Body/Cardio)
    Product(s) Used: N/A
    Detailed Action: Engage in 60-75 minutes of intense full-body workout or cardio, including warm-up and cool-down. Focus on precision and effort.
    Frequency: 3x per week (e.g., Mon, Wed, Fri)
  • 5. Travel to Shower/Home
    Product(s) Used: N/A
    Detailed Action: Efficiently travel from gym back home/to shower. Minimize downtime.
    Frequency: Daily (if gym is not at home)
Phase 3: The Shower Ritual – Hair, Body, Shave & Body Hair Removal (5:35 AM - 6:00 AM)
  • 6. Hair Thinning Targeted Shampoo
    Product(s) Used: Bumble and Bumble Full Potential Hair Preserving Shampoo (or Nizoral Anti-Dandruff Shampoo 2% Ketoconazole for dandruff)
    Detailed Action: Apply to wet hair, massage vigorously into scalp for 1-2 minutes to stimulate blood flow and allow active ingredients to penetrate. Rinse thoroughly.
    Frequency: Daily (or alternate Nizoral 2-3x/week)
  • 7. Hair Preserving Conditioner
    Product(s) Used: Bumble and Bumble Full Potential Hair Preserving Conditioner
    Detailed Action: Apply from mid-lengths to ends, leave for 1-2 minutes. Rinse thoroughly.
    Frequency: Daily
  • 8. Body Cleansing & Exfoliation (Prep for Hair Removal)
    Product(s) Used: Aesop - Geranium Leaf Body Cleanser (Daily); Fresh - Brown Sugar Body Polish (2-3x/week)
    Detailed Action: Lather daily body cleanser. On days you will shave/trim body hair, use the body polish generously on targeted areas to lift hairs and remove dead skin. Rinse thoroughly.
    Frequency: Daily (Exfoliation: 2-3x/week)
  • 9. Body Hair Trimming & Shaving
    Product(s) Used: Body Groomer (e.g., Manscaped Lawn Mower, Philips Norelco Bodygroom); Multi-Blade Razor (e.g., Gillette Fusion5); Shaving Gel/Cream (e.g., Cremo Cooling Shave Cream)
    Detailed Action: **Prep:** If hair is long, dry trim first. **In Shower:** Apply shaving gel/cream. **Shave:** Chest/Stomach/Shoulders/Back/Arms/Legs: Trim with 0.5-1mm guard, then shave against grain for smooth. **Trim:** Armpits/Groin: Use 1-6mm guard. Rinse thoroughly. (Refer to detailed instructions for specific areas/lengths for optimal cleanliness and minimal irritation).
    Frequency: Shaving: Every 1-3 days (as needed); Trimming: Weekly/Bi-weekly
  • 10. Hot Towel & Face Shave Prep
    Product(s) Used: Truefitt & Hill - Ultimate Comfort Shaving Cream (or Proraso - Shaving Cream)
    Detailed Action: While in shower, wet small towel with hot water, apply to face for 30-60 seconds. Immediately apply shaving cream and shave meticulously.
    Frequency: Daily (if shaving face daily)
  • 11. Cold Water Rinse & Post-Shave Balm (Face)
    Product(s) Used: Proraso - After Shave Balm (Sensitive Skin, Green Tea & Oat)
    Detailed Action: Splash face with cold water. Pat dry. Apply small amount of balm to shaved areas, gently patting.
    Frequency: Daily
Phase 4: Post-Shower Skincare Ritual – Precision Layering (6:00 AM - 6:30 AM)
  • 12. Post-Body Hair Removal Soothe & Prevent Ingrowns
    Product(s) Used: Tend Skin Solution (or Fur Ingrown Concentrate)
    Detailed Action: Apply immediately to all areas where body hair was removed with a cotton pad or fingertips.
    Frequency: On body hair removal days
  • 13. Body Moisturizer
    Product(s) Used: Aesop - Geranium Leaf Body Balm (or Kiehl's - Crème de Corps)
    Detailed Action: Apply generously over entire body, ensuring all skin is hydrated and smooth.
    Frequency: Daily
  • 14. Scalp Treatment (Leave-On)
    Product(s) Used: Topical Minoxidil (e.g., Rogaine 5% Foam/Liquid) or The Ordinary - Multi-Peptide Serum for Hair Density (or Bumble and Bumble Full Potential Hair Preserving Booster Hair Mist)
    Detailed Action: Towel-dry hair lightly. Apply directly to thinning scalp areas, massage gently. Do NOT rinse.
    Frequency: Daily (Minoxidil 2x/day recommended if used)
  • 15. Essence/First Treatment Essence
    Product(s) Used: SK-II - Facial Treatment Essence (or Cosrx - Advanced Snail 96 Mucin Power Essence)
    Detailed Action: Pour a few drops into palms, gently press onto entire face and neck.
    Frequency: Daily
  • 16. Hydrating Toner
    Product(s) Used: Kiehl's - Calendula Herbal Extract Alcohol-Free Toner (or Skin1004 Toner Pad)
    Detailed Action: Apply with a quick swipe (pad) or gentle pat (liquid) over face and neck.
    Frequency: Daily
  • 17. Primary Antioxidant Serum
    Product(s) Used: SkinCeuticals - C E Ferulic
    Detailed Action: Apply 3-5 drops to face, neck, and décolletage. Press gently. Allow 30-60 seconds to absorb.
    Frequency: Daily
  • 18. Soothing/Hydrating Ampoule
    Product(s) Used: Skin1004 Madagascar Centella Ampoule
    Detailed Action: Apply a few drops and gently pat onto face. Allow to absorb.
    Frequency: Daily
  • 19. Targeted Peptide Serum
    Product(s) Used: The Ordinary - Multi-Peptide + HA Serum
    Detailed Action: Apply a few drops, focusing on areas of concern (e.g., fine lines). Pat in.
    Frequency: Daily
  • 20. Eye Serum (Layer 1)
    Product(s) Used: Drunk Elephant - A-Shaba Complex Eye Serum
    Detailed Action: Apply small amount around orbital bone, gently patting with ring finger.
    Frequency: Daily
  • 21. Eye Cream (Layer 2)
    Product(s) Used: Your existing Japanese Undereye Cream (or Shiseido - Men Total Revitalizer Eye)
    Detailed Action: Apply small, rice-grain sized amount over serum, pat until absorbed.
    Frequency: Daily
  • 22. Moisturizer
    Product(s) Used: Your existing Skin1004 Soothing Cream (or Clinique - For Men Maximum Hydrator 72-Hour Auto-Replenishing Hydrator)
    Detailed Action: Apply an even layer over face and neck.
    Frequency: Daily
  • 23. High-SPF Broad-Spectrum Sunscreen (The Ultimate Shield)
    Product(s) Used: Supergoop! - Unseen Sunscreen SPF 40
    Detailed Action: Apply generous amount (at least a quarter-teaspoon for face alone) as the very last step. Blend thoroughly and quickly.
    Frequency: Daily (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
Phase 5: Oral & Final Prep (6:30 AM - 7:00 AM)
  • 24. Advanced Oral Hygiene
    Product(s) Used: Marvis - Whitening Mint Toothpaste; Oral-B - iO Series Electric Toothbrush; Listerine (or BreathRx - Anti-Bacterial Mouth Rinse)
    Detailed Action: Brush meticulously (electric toothbrush maximizes efficiency). Use mouthwash. Consider water flosser if desired for deeper clean.
    Frequency: Daily
  • 25. Dress & Hair Styling
    Product(s) Used: N/A
    Detailed Action: Dress quickly (clothes laid out previous night for efficiency). Perform any quick hair styling.
    Frequency: Daily
  • 26. Quick Breakfast/Coffee & Final Mirror Check
    Product(s) Used: N/A
    Detailed Action: Consume pre-made smoothie, protein bar, or quick coffee. Perform a final, discerning look in the mirror for any imperfections.
    Frequency: Daily
  • 27. Gather Work Items & Commute
    Product(s) Used: N/A
    Detailed Action: Ensure all necessary items for work are gathered. Commence commute to arrive before 8:00 AM.
    Frequency: Daily
